# SJW Scouts Website

Official website for **1st St. John’s West Scout Group**
Live Site: [https://sjwscouts.pages.dev](https://sjwscouts.pages.dev)

---

## Overview

This website was created to support youth membership growth and volunteer recruitment for:

* **Beaver Colony** (Grades K–2)
* **Cub Pack** (Grades 3–5)

The site is designed to provide clear, transparent information for parents and prospective volunteers about:

* Program details
* Meeting times and locations
* Registration dates
* Membership costs
* Volunteer expectations
* Camp opportunities
* Contact information

---

## Key Registration Information

* **New youth registrations open:** June 1, 2026
* **All new youth members begin programming:** September 2026
* **Volunteer registration:** Open year-round through MyScouts.ca

---

## Main Goals

### Youth Recruitment

Help local families understand:

* What Scouting offers
* Program structure
* Costs and expectations
* How to register

### Volunteer Recruitment

Encourage parents and community members to become Scouters by outlining:

* Training requirements
* Time commitments
* Screening process
* Leadership opportunities
