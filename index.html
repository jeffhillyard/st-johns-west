Who We Are

A community going strong for over four decades.

Formerly 1st Cowan Heights Scout Group, we're a volunteer-run Scouts Canada group meeting weekly in the west end of St. John's. Our program is built around fun, friendship, and growing good humans.

Mission

Help develop well-rounded youth, better prepared for success in the world.

Vision

Canadian youth making a meaningful contribution to creating a better world.

🎒

A note on camping gear

Campers will need basic personal gear — a sleeping bag and a sleeping pad or air mattress are the main essentials. That said, we encourage families not to spend a lot on camping equipment, especially at this age. A basic sleeping bag and a simple foam pad are perfectly fine. If you're unsure what to grab, just ask us — we're happy to point you in the right direction without breaking the bank.

Get Involved

We need you — and it's more fun than you think.

Our program is entirely run by adult volunteers called "Scouters." Full training is provided — no prior experience necessary. We genuinely cannot run without parent support.

🎓

Online Training

15–20 hours at your own pace. Must be completed within 90 days of registering as a Scouter.

📅

Weekly Meetings

Just 1 hour per week, plus ~30 minutes prep time for meeting materials.

⛺

Weekend Camps

3–4 camps per year — each a 1 or 2 night overnight at our group's own cabin.

🔍

Background Check

All Scouters complete a PRC/VSC background check through the RNC — required to protect all youth in our care.

💡 Don't wait until September! Adult volunteer registration on MyScouts.ca is open year-round. Give yourself 3–4 weeks for the background check and online training to be processed before the first meeting.
